<p><p>The Bleached Chemical Mechanical Pulp (BCMP) market includes two primary types: Chemi-Mechanical Pulp (CMP) and Chemi-Thermo-Mechanical Pulp (CTMP). CMP combines chemical and mechanical processes to efficiently produce pulp, resulting in higher yield and better quality for paper products. CTMP, on the other hand, incorporates a thermal stage to enhance fiber properties and reduce energy consumption. Both types aim to provide environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional pulping methods, catering to the growing demand for sustainable paper production.</p></p>
