<p><p>The disc plow market comprises primarily two types: flat disc disc plows and conical disc plows. Flat disc disc plows feature broad, flat blades that efficiently cut through soil and are ideal for shallow tillage, especially in lighter soils. In contrast, conical disc plows have curved, tapered blades designed for deeper penetration and heavy-duty tasks, making them suitable for tougher soil conditions. Both types enhance soil aeration and preparation, catering to varying agricultural needs and farming practices.</p></p>
