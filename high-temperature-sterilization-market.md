Steam sterilization, utilizing saturated steam under pressure, is effective for heat and moisture-resistant items. Dry sterilization employs hot air to eliminate microorganisms, suitable for materials susceptible to moisture. Other methods may involve advanced techniques such as ethylene oxide or plasma sterilization. Each method serves specific applications across healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and laboratories, ensuring the safety and sterility of tools, instruments, and products.</p></p>
