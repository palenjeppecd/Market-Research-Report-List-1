<p><ul><li>WiFi</li><li>DECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video doorbell market is primarily categorized into WiFi and DECT types. WiFi video doorbells connect to home networks via wireless internet, enabling remote viewing and two-way communication through smartphones or tablets. They offer high flexibility and integration with smart home systems. In contrast, DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) video doorbells use a dedicated radio frequency for secure and stable connections, ideal for areas with poor WiFi. Both types enhance home security and convenience while catering to varying user preferences.</p></p>
