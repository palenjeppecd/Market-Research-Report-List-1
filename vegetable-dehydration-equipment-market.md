<p><p>The vegetable dehydration equipment market comprises two main types: continuous and intermittent dehydration equipment. Continuous dehydration equipment operates in a constant flow, allowing for the simultaneous processing of large volumes of vegetables. This type is efficient for high-demand operations. In contrast, intermittent dehydration equipment processes batches of vegetables at a time, making it more suitable for smaller production needs or varied product lines. Both types cater to different scales of operation and production requirements in the food processing industry.</p></p>
