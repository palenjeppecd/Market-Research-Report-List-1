<p><strong>The Mask Inspection Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the mask inspection equipment market, the Die to Die (DD) method involves comparing masks directly against previously verified reference patterns, ensuring high fidelity in defect detection. This method is crucial in semiconductor manufacturing for maintaining yield and quality. Conversely, the Die to Database (DB) method inspects masks by referencing a digital library of known good patterns, enabling rapid detection and classification of defects. Both methodologies are essential for ensuring mask integrity and optimizing production processes in advanced lithography.</p></p>
