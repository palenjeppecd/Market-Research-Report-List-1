<p><strong>The Cutting Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cooling</li><li>Lubrication</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cutting fluid market is primarily categorized into two types: cooling fluids and lubrication fluids. Cooling fluids are designed to dissipate heat generated during machining, preventing tool wear and maintaining the integrity of the workpiece. In contrast, lubrication fluids minimize friction between cutting tools and the material, enhancing tool life and surface finish. Both types play crucial roles in improving machining efficiency and performance, ensuring optimal conditions for various manufacturing processes across industries.</p></p>
