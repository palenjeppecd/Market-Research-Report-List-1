<p><p>The dust-proof material market includes several key types, each designed for specific applications. Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) provides exceptional chemical resistance and low friction properties. Aramid fibers are known for their high strength and thermal stability, making them ideal for protective clothing. Each material plays a vital role in various dust-proof applications.</p></p>
