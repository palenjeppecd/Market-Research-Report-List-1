<p><p>The pressure relief regulator valve market includes several types, each serving unique applications. Diaphragm types utilize a flexible membrane for precise pressure control, while spring film types rely on a spring mechanism to regulate pressure levels. Piston types use a movable piston for quick response to pressure changes, and leveraged types employ a lever system for enhanced force transfer. Lastly, bellows types feature a flexible bellows element that expands or contracts to maintain pressure stability, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
