<p><p>The End Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) treatment market comprises three primary modalities: hemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis, and kidney transplantation. Hemodialysis involves utilizing a machine to filter waste from the blood, typically performed in clinics or at home. Peritoneal dialysis uses the lining of the abdomen to filter blood, allowing for greater patient flexibility and independence. Kidney transplantation involves surgically implanting a healthy kidney from a donor, providing the best chance for a long-term solution and improved quality of life.</p></p>
