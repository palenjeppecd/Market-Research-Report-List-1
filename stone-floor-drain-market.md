<p><strong>The Stone Floor Drain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exposed Floor Drain</li><li>Concealed Floor Drain</li><li>Inset Floor Drain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stone floor drain market encompasses three main types: exposed, concealed, and inset floor drains. Exposed floor drains are visible and often integrate with the flooring design, serving both function and aesthetics. Concealed floor drains are hidden from view, providing a seamless look while ensuring effective drainage. Inset floor drains are flush with the floor surface, allowing for easy cleaning and minimal disruption to the floor’s appearance. Each type caters to different design preferences and functional requirements in various settings.</p></p>
