<p><p>The Terpene Distillation Kit market features two primary types: microwave distillation and steam distillation. Microwave distillation utilizes microwave energy to heat plant materials rapidly, leading to efficient terpene extraction while minimizing degradation. In contrast, steam distillation employs steam to vaporize volatile compounds, allowing for the separation of terpenes from the plant matrix. Both methods cater to the demand for high-quality terpene extraction in various industries, including cosmetics, food, and pharmaceuticals, highlighting their distinct techniques and benefits.</p></p>
