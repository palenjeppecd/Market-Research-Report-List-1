<p><strong>The Air Suspension Control System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capsule</li><li>Membrane</li><li>Compound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air suspension control system market is categorized into three main types: capsule, membrane, and compound systems. Capsule systems utilize sealed air-filled chambers for cushioning, providing a smooth ride with minimal maintenance. Membrane systems, on the other hand, employ flexible membranes that adjust air volume and pressure for real-time responsiveness, enhancing comfort and stability. Compound systems combine features of both capsule and membrane types, offering improved versatility and performance in varying load conditions, making them suitable for diverse applications.</p></p>
