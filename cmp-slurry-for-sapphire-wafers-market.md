<p><p>CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing) slurry for sapphire wafers is essential for achieving smooth surfaces during semiconductor manufacturing. The two primary types are colloidal silica polishing slurry and alumina polishing slurry. Colloidal silica slurry provides fine polishing and is suitable for achieving ultra-smooth finishes, while alumina polishing slurry offers aggressive material removal and is effective for initial wafer shaping. Both types enhance the efficiency and quality of sapphire wafers used in various electronic and optoelectronic applications.</p></p>
