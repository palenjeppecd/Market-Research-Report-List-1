<p><p>Smart coatings encompass various specialized types tailored for specific protective and functional applications. Anti-corrosion coatings protect surfaces from oxidation and degradation. Self-cleaning coatings utilize hydrophobic properties to repel dirt and grime, while self-healing coatings can automatically repair surface damage, extending the longevity and effectiveness of materials.</p></p>
