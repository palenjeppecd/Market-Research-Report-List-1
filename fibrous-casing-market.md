<p><p>The fibrous casing market features two main types: regular fibrous casing and peelable fibrous casing. Regular fibrous casing is a robust, non-edible option primarily used for encasing various meat products, providing structural integrity and moisture retention during processing. Peelable fibrous casing, on the other hand, offers convenience and is designed to be removed easily after cooking, enhancing presentation and texture. Both types cater to different processing needs, suitable for a range of products in the food industry.</p></p>
