<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Extender Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber Type</li><li>Multimode Fiber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber extender market is primarily categorized into two types: Single Mode Fiber (SMF) and Multimode Fiber (MMF). Single Mode Fiber is designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light wave, which allows for higher bandwidth and better performance over extended ranges. In contrast, Multimode Fiber is suitable for shorter distances, transmitting multiple light rays through a larger core, making it ideal for local area networks and data centers. Both types cater to specific networking needs based on distance and data transmission requirements.</p></p>
