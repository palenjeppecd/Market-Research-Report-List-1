<p><p>A Gas Engine-driven Heat Pump (GEHP) is a heating and cooling system that utilizes a natural gas engine to drive the heat pump's compressor, providing an efficient alternative to traditional electric heat pumps. GEHP systems are particularly valued for their ability to deliver high heating capacities while being environmentally friendly, as they produce lower greenhouse gas emissions compared to conventional fossil fuel systems.</p><p>The GEHP market is poised for noteworthy developments, driven by increasing energy efficiency regulations and a shift towards renewable energy sources.
