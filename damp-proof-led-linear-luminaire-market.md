<p><p>The Damp-Proof LED Linear Luminaire market comprises various ingress protection (IP) ratings, including IP64, IP65, IP66, IP67, and IP68. Each rating indicates the luminaire's resistance to dust and water. IP64 offers protection against dust and splashes; IP65 is dust-tight and can withstand low-pressure water jets; IP66 withstands powerful water jets; IP67 is waterproof temporarily under submersion; and IP68 is designed for continuous underwater use. This ensures optimal performance in various challenging environments, enhancing safety and longevity.</p></p>
