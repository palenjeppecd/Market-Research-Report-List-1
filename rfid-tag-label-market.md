<p><strong>The RFID Tag & Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID tag and label market is broadly categorized into two types: active and passive. Active RFID tags have their own power source, enabling longer read ranges and the storage of more data, making them ideal for tracking high-value assets over greater distances. Conversely, passive RFID tags rely on energy from the reader signal, making them cost-effective and suitable for high-volume applications, such as inventory management and access control, but with shorter read ranges. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries.</p></p>
