<p><p>The Dairy Separation and Filtration Devices market encompasses two primary types: separation systems and filtration systems. Separation systems focus on dividing components of milk, such as fat and proteins, using centrifugation or membrane technologies to obtain specific dairy products like cream or whey. Filtration systems, on the other hand, utilize processes like microfiltration and ultrafiltration to remove pathogens and enhance product purity, extending shelf life while retaining essential nutrients. Both systems are vital for improving efficiency and quality in dairy processing.</p></p>
