<p><p>The silicone diffusion pump fluids market is categorized into low viscosity and high viscosity silicone oils. Low viscosity silicone oils are characterized by their fluidity, facilitating efficient vapor transport in vacuum applications, making them suitable for systems requiring quick pump-down times. Conversely, high viscosity silicone oils provide enhanced thermal stability and are often utilized in high-temperature environments, offering superior pumping performance in demanding vacuum systems. Both types are essential for optimizing vacuum technologies across various industrial applications.</p></p>
