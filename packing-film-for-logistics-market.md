<p><p>Packing film for logistics encompasses various types, primarily stretch wrapping and shrink wrapping. Stretch wrapping involves tightly wrapping plastic film around products on a pallet, securing them for stability during transport. It's flexible and allows for easy adjustments. In contrast, shrink wrapping involves encasing products in a plastic film that shrinks when heat is applied, creating a snug, protective seal. Both methods enhance product safety, reduce damage, and improve efficiency during shipping and storage in the logistics market.</p></p>
