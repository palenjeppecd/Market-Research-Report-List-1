<p><strong>The Solar-powered Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Suction</li><li>Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar-powered pump market primarily consists of two types: surface suction pumps and submersible pumps. Surface suction pumps draw water from a direct source, such as a river or pond, and are typically used for irrigation or livestock watering in shallow water scenarios. In contrast, submersible pumps are designed to be submerged underwater, pushing water to the surface, making them suitable for deeper wells and boreholes. Both types capitalize on solar energy, promoting energy efficiency and sustainability in water management practices.</p></p>
