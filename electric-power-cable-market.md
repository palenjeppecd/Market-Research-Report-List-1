<p><p>The electric power cable market is categorized into several types based on voltage levels. Low Voltage Cables (up to 1 kV) are used for residential applications; Medium Voltage Cables (1 kV to 33 kV) support industrial and commercial setups. High Voltage Cables (33 kV to 230 kV) transmit electricity over long distances, while Extra High Voltage Cables (230 kV to 765 kV) facilitate large-scale transmission in power grids. Ultra High Voltage (UHV) Cables, exceeding 765 kV, enable efficient long-distance power transport, minimizing losses.</p></p>
