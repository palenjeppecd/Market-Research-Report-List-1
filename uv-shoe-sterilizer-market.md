<p><p>The UV Shoe Sterilizer market includes two main types: in-shoe disinfection and sole disinfection. Sole disinfection targets the outer part of the shoe, effectively sanitizing the sole to prevent the transfer of harmful germs to various surfaces. Both methods utilize ultraviolet light technology to ensure a hygienic footwear solution, promoting overall health and cleanliness.</p></p>
