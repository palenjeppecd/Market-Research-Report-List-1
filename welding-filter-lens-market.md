<p><p>The welding filter lens market is divided into two main types: transparent lenses and opaque lenses. Transparent lenses allow light transmission while offering protection against harmful radiation, making them ideal for tasks requiring visibility and precision, such as inspection and light welding. Opaque lenses, on the other hand, filter out most visible light, providing greater protection during high-intensity welding operations. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring safety and comfort for welders by minimizing eye strain and exposure to hazardous environments.</p></p>
