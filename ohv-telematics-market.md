<p><p>The OHV (Off-Highway Vehicle) telematics market encompasses two primary types: cellular and satellite. It is cost-effective and ideal for regions with established networks. Satellite telematics, on the other hand, provides global coverage regardless of network availability, making it suitable for remote areas. Both types enhance vehicle management, safety, and tracking, catering to varying operational needs in the OHV sector.</p></p>
