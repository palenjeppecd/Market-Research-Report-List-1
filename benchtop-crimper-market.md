<p><p>The benchtop crimper market is divided into two primary types: pneumatics and electric. Pneumatic crimpers utilize compressed air to achieve high force and speed, making them ideal for high-volume production settings. They offer reliability and can handle various materials. Conversely, electric crimpers employ electric motors for operation, providing precision and ease of use. These are suitable for lower volume tasks and offer versatility in applications, often incorporating features like adjustable settings for different wire sizes.</p></p>
