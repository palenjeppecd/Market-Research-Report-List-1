<p><p>Captive power generation encompasses various market types based on energy production efficiency and utility. </p><p>1. **Cogeneration** generates electricity and useful heat simultaneously, optimizing fuel use. </p><p>2. **Tri-Generation** expands on this by adding cooling, providing electricity, heat, and chilled water for air conditioning. </p><p>3. **Quad-Generation** further enhances efficiency by incorporating additional processes, such as water desalination. </p><p>4. The **normal market** refers to traditional electricity purchasing from utility providers without integrated thermal or cooling solutions, typically less efficient than captive systems.</p></p>
