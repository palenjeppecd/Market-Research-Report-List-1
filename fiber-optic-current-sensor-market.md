<p><p>The Fiber Optic Current Sensor market is primarily categorized into DC and AC types based on their operational capabilities. DC type sensors are designed to measure direct current levels, utilizing the Faraday effect to detect changes in the magnetic field generated by the current. In contrast, AC type sensors are tailored for alternating currents, effectively monitoring varying current levels and frequencies. Both types leverage fiber optic technology for enhanced sensitivity and immunity to electromagnetic interference, making them suitable for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
