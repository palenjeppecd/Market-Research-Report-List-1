<p><p>The screen fingerprint sensor market is mainly divided into two types: swipe sensors and area sensors. Swipe sensors require users to drag their finger across a designated area on the screen to capture the fingerprint. In contrast, area sensors capture the entire fingerprint image in one go, allowing for quicker and more efficient recognition. Both types cater to the increasing demand for secure authentication in smartphones and other devices, enhancing user experience and security in mobile technology.</p></p>
