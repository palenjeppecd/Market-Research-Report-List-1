Extended Densified Kraft (XL) provides added flexibility and durability, suitable for high-performance scenarios. Polycoated Kraft (PCK) features a moisture-resistant coating, enhancing its suitability for food packaging. Extensible Polycoated Kraft (EK) combines stretchability with a protective layer, catering to industries requiring both flexibility and resistance to punctures and moisture.</p></p>
