<p><p>The household ventilation fan market comprises three primary types: ceiling fans, wall-mounted fans, and window-mounted fans. Ceiling fans circulate air efficiently in larger spaces and are popular for their aesthetic appeal and energy efficiency. Wall-mounted fans are ideal for smaller rooms or areas with limited floor space, providing focused airflow without taking up valuable space. Window-mounted fans are designed to be placed in open windows, allowing fresh air circulation while expelling stale air, making them effective for cross-ventilation.</p></p>
