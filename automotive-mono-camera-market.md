<p><strong>The Automotive Mono Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive mono camera market primarily includes two main sensor types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors offer high image quality and low noise, making them suitable for applications requiring precision, such as night driving. However, they consume more power and are generally more expensive. CMOS sensors, on the other hand, are increasingly popular due to their lower cost, reduced power consumption, and enhanced integration capabilities, making them ideal for advanced driver-assistance systems and other automotive applications.</p></p>
