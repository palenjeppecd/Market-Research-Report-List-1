<p><p>The Noninvasive Positive-pressure Ventilation (NIPPV) market consists of various devices designed to assist breathing without invasive procedures. Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) ventilators maintain constant airflow to keep airways open, primarily used for sleep apnea. Automatic Positive Airway Pressure (autoPAP) ventilators adjust pressure levels based on patient needs throughout the night. Dual Horizontal Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) ventilators provide two distinct pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation, enhancing comfort and efficacy. Other devices in the market may include specialized or portable ventilators.</p></p>
