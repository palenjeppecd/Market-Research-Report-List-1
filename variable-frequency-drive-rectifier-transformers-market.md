<p><p>The Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) Rectifier Transformers market is divided into two main types: liquid-filled and dry-type transformers. Liquid-filled transformers use insulating oil for cooling and electrical insulation, offering high efficiency and better thermal performance, suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, dry-type transformers utilize air for cooling, providing lower maintenance needs and enhanced safety due to reduced fire risk. Both types play a crucial role in converting variable frequency power for industrial and commercial applications, each with distinct advantages based on operational requirements.</p></p>
