<p><p>The Bone Growth Stimulating Device market consists of three main types: Inductive Coupling Therapy, Capacitive Coupling Therapy, and Combined Therapy. Inductive Coupling Therapy utilizes electromagnetic fields to promote bone healing by inducing electrical currents in the tissue. Capacitive Coupling Therapy employs electrical fields to stimulate cell regeneration and enhance healing processes. Combined Therapy integrates both techniques, optimizing the healing environment by leveraging the strengths of each method, making it versatile for various orthopedic conditions and enhancing overall treatment efficacy.</p></p>
