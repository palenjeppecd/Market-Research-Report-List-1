<p><p>Fiber to the X (FTTx) market encompasses various deployment models that leverage fiber optic technology for enhanced connectivity. Fiber to the Home/Premises/Building (FTTh/p/b) delivers high-speed internet directly to residential or commercial spaces, ensuring optimal performance. Fiber to the Node/Curb (FTTn/c) extends fiber to a neighborhood node or curb, connecting to existing copper lines, improving urban broadband access. Fiber to the Antenna (FTTa) links fiber directly to cellular towers, enhancing mobile network performance and supporting the increasing demand for data.</p></p>
