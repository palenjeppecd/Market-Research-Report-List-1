<p><p>The dental dam market primarily consists of two types: latex and nitrile. Latex dental dams are made from natural rubber and are favored for their elasticity and cost-effectiveness, providing an effective barrier during dental procedures. Nitrile dental dams, on the other hand, are synthetic and offer enhanced durability, puncture resistance, and are ideal for individuals with latex allergies. Both types serve essential roles in maintaining hygiene and preventing cross-contamination in dental practices, catering to varying user needs and preferences.</p></p>
