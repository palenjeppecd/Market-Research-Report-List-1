<p><p>The Self-Monitoring of Blood Glucose (SMBG) device market includes three primary components: strips, glucose meters, and lancets. Strips are used to measure glucose levels from a blood sample, providing critical data for diabetes management. Glucose meters display the readings from the strips, allowing users to track their blood sugar levels over time. Lancets are small needles used to obtain blood samples. Together, these devices empower individuals to monitor their glucose levels effectively and manage their diabetes proactively.</p></p>
