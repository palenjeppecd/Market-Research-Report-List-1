<p><p>Affiliate software can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed directly on a company's servers, providing full control over data and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based affiliate software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs with subscription-based pricing. This model allows organizations to access the software from anywhere and typically includes automatic updates and support, catering to diverse business needs.</p></p>
