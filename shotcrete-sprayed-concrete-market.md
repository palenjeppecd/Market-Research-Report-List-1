<p><ul><li>Dry Mix</li><li>Wet Mix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shotcrete and sprayed concrete market consists of two primary types: dry mix and wet mix. Dry mix shotcrete involves blending dry materials that are later mixed with water during spraying, offering control over the moisture content and faster setting times. In contrast, wet mix shotcrete involves pre-mixing all components, including water, before delivery to the spraying nozzle, ensuring a uniform mixture and allowing for better workability. Both methods are widely used in construction, tunneling, and repair projects for enhanced durability and efficiency.</p></p>
