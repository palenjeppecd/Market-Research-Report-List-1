<p><p>The non-woven disc market is primarily divided into two main types: quick change type and arbor hole type. Quick change discs are designed for easy attachment and removal, enhancing efficiency in applications where frequent swaps are necessary. Arbor hole discs, on the other hand, are characterized by a central hole that fits onto specific tools, offering stability during operation. Both types serve various industrial purposes, with their selection largely depending on the criteria of convenience, tool compatibility, and specific tasks at hand.</p></p>
