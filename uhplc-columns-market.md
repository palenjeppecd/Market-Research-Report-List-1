<p><strong>The UHPLC Columns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Packed Columns</li><li>Capillary Columns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UHPLC columns market is primarily divided into two types: packed columns and capillary columns. Packed columns contain small particles packed tightly, allowing for high-resolution separations and efficiencies ideal for complex samples. They are widely used in pharmaceuticals and biochemistry. Capillary columns, on the other hand, have a narrow diameter and are suited for specialized applications requiring minimal sample volumes and high sensitivity, such as proteomics and metabolomics. Both types are essential for modern analytical chemistry.</p></p>
