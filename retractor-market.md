<p><ul><li>Hand Held Retractors</li><li>Self-Retaining Retractors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The retractor market comprises two main types: hand-held and self-retaining retractors. Hand-held retractors require surgical staff to hold them in place, allowing for temporary exposure during procedures. These instruments are typically versatile and come in various shapes and sizes. In contrast, self-retaining retractors are designed to hold tissue open without manual support, enhancing surgical efficiency by freeing up the hands of the surgeon. Both types are vital in improving visibility and access during surgical interventions.</p></p>
